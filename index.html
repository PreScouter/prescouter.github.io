What We Don't Do

Deep Expertise

We don't provide experts with a long history in a niche. We take bright minds from a range of disciplines and apply them to a challenge, without the baggage of preconceived notions.

Prototyping

We don't build products. We provide information and consultation, which could include a comparison of who might best build what you are looking for.

Long Learning Curve

Our team works in the background and requires only three to four hours of the client's time. If the challenge can not be articulated within an hour phone conversation, it is probably not a good fit.
