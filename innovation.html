Some of our most requested projects:

Technology Landscapes

Technology Landscapes provide a comprehensive overview of startups, researchers, patents and technologies as it relates to your area, including adjacent markets & industries. Technology Landscapes generally curate information in a manner that provides data for analysis, producing a number of entries that have direct or adjacent applicability to the technical domain.

 

Technology Scouting

Technology Scouting provides a balance between curation and analysis. Technology Scouting reports scan the entire globe, spanning startups, researchers, patents and technologies as it relates to your technical challenge. The Scholar team will analyze and refine technologies based on your feedback. Upon identifying interesting technologies, PreScouter will launch an in-depth investigation on a number of technologies, including answers to your specific questions. You stay anonymous while we address your technical questions. Technology Scouting reports provide strong value balancing analysis with coverage, honing in on specific technologies for a detailed investigation.

 

Market Analysis

A Market Analysis report answers what are the key factors will enable growth. Common topics for market analyses include - Identify new markets for my existing product(s); perform a market analysis & survey data in emerging markets; will my product thrive in a particular market segment?

PreScouter will integrate market size, technology growth factors and barriers to entry as a means of understanding a target market or domain.

 

Competitive Analysis

Understanding your product's ecosystem is critical to maintaining a competitive advantage. We research on your behalf, providing you with analysis that is enables you to make an informed decision.

 

Business Plans & Grant Proposals

PreScouter can provide you with support for your next big idea. Validate your idea ensure you understand the competition, analyze the market for growth potential. If you already have done your research, let us help you prepare the presentation, grant proposal, we have decades of combined experience behind us.

 

Startup Interviews

The ability to attain unbiased information is at PreScouter's core. Startups and nascent technologies need to be evaluated beyond the hype. Understanding what is real and what is good marketing is how PreScouter helps our clients every day. Either provide us with your questions, or conduct the interview with PreScouter as a proxy. No matter what you choose, you'll hear information from the source.

How Our Service works

Step 1

Tell us your criteria for the people, organizations or technologies you want profiled.

Step 2

We’ll present profiles that meet your criteria. You select the ones of most interest. What questions do you have for each profile?

Step 3

Through interviews and analysis, we’ll build enhanced profiles that answer your questions. We can even connect you to the interviewees.
