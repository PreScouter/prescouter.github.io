Careers

Get your career on track with a fast growing company

 

What does a career with PreScouter mean?
  • Fast Growth

    PreScouter is growing month on month, requiring exceptional people to step up and take on greater responsibility. The career opportunities are endless.

  • Challenging Work

    PreScouter is continuously improving its systems, processes and services. A career at PreScouter is more than a 9 to 5 job: its an opportunity to develop your craft, take ownership for an important part of the business and to grow both professionally and personally..

  • Support

    We believe your success will in part be a result of the support systems we provide for you. When we add to our team, we work hard to ensure each new team member has the resources he or she needs to be successful – whether it be support staff, software or training.

What do we look for?
  • Passion

    We look for people who believe in what we do.

  • Self Motivated

    Our team is located across the world, as are our clients and the PhDs at universities we work with. You have to be comfortable working independently as necessary, as well as working self-directed towards the goals agreed with the rest of the team.

  • Process Oriented

    As a fast growing company, we need people who can build, follow and train the processes we develop for scaling the business.
