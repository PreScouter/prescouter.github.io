PreScouter will give you an overview of the relevant IP around your new invention or area of interest so you can make an informed decision on where or what to invest.

IP / Patent Landscapes

An intellectual property (IP) landscape identifies a domain of legal interest so as to develop a conceptual framework for that space. Your PreScouter project team will perform an exhaustive global search to identify patents, both with direct , and tangential relevance to your domain of interest. Our deliverables include an interactive landscape map as well as a full report with analysis, custom-tailored to your needs.

 

Whitespace analysis

A white space analysis exams the hidden data. PreScouter designs each white space analysis by understanding the seen and unseen variables around a specific domain. Your Scholar team analyzes the space for trends, identifying the nascent opportunities that exist within a market space. We provide your team with a visual and analytical view of a particular domain - which ideas are trending, and equally important, which ideas will be trending in the near future.
